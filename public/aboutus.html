Welcome to WERGHI'S Hotel

Founded in 2010, WERGHI'S Hotel has been redefining luxury hospitality in the heart of the city. What began as a modest 20-room establishment has blossomed into one of the region's most prestigious destinations, renowned for our impeccable service and attention to detail.

Our philosophy is simple: every guest deserves to be treated like royalty. From the moment you step through our doors, our dedicated team is committed to creating unforgettable experiences tailored to your individual needs.
